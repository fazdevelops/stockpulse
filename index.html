How to use StockPulse for spot trading
STEP 1 — FIRST-TIME SETUP
Click ⚙ in the top right to open Settings. Enter your Finnhub key (live prices) and Alpha Vantage key (historical data). Both are free and take 30 seconds to get. Set your portfolio amount.
STEP 2 — DAILY ROUTINE
Open this file each morning before market open. Select a universe (or your Watchlist). Press Run Scan. Focus on BUY signals with a high score (4–5 pips filled) and multiple green flags.
STEP 3 — READING THE SIGNALS
50>200 EMA — Uptrend. Bullish momentum confirmed.
RSI oversold — RSI <30. Potential reversal. Strong buy zone.
Vol spike — Volume >1.5×. Big players are active — confirms the move.
MACD bull — Momentum turning positive.
BB lower — Price near lower Bollinger Band — reversal zone.
STEP 4 — PLACING YOUR TRADE
Click Detail ↗ on any row. The trade plan shows exactly what to do:

1. Place a limit buy order at Entry Price
2. Buy exactly the position size shown
3. Immediately place a stop-limit sell at Stop-Loss
4. Place a limit sell (take-profit) at Target

Click Copy Trade Plan to copy it for your broker notes.
⚠ BEGINNER RULES
Never risk more than 2% per trade · Always set stop-loss before entering · Don't chase stocks already up 3%+ · Start with 1–2 trades at a time · Use Backtesting to check how reliable signals have been historically · This tool is for analysis only, not financial advice.
📱 USE ON YOUR PHONE — Add to Home Screen
Step 1 — Upload the file to iCloud Drive or Google Drive
On your computer, upload StockPulse-Pro-v3.html to your iCloud Drive or Google Drive.

Step 2 — Open on your iPhone
Open the Files app → find the file in iCloud Drive → tap to open it. It opens in Safari automatically.

Step 3 — Add to Home Screen
In Safari, tap the Share button (box with arrow at bottom) → scroll down → tap "Add to Home Screen" → tap Add.

Step 4 — Done ✓
A StockPulse icon appears on your home screen. Tap it anytime — it opens fullscreen like a real app. Your API keys and settings are saved automatically.

On Android: Open file in Chrome → tap the 3-dot menu → "Add to Home screen".
